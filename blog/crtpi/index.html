INTRO

I recently got a Raspberry Pi 3B to connect to my CRT. it turns out a pi is actually super cool and can do a lot of stuff. i highly recommend it if you have a crt. the pi 3b has a composite output and with some settings, it looks really good.


also this post is kinda a sneak peak into what the "RETRO ZONE" will be, I love CRTs and want to make a shrine-like page about my crt setup

WHAT IT DOES

ive been using retropie along with kodi and moonlight to do pretty much anything i want. Retropie lets me emulate pretty much anything up to the SNES era of consoles. it can do n64 but ive heard it gives mixed results and i have a everdrive anyway.


Kodi connects to my Plex server so I can watch all my old movies/anime/tv (AKIRA!!!!) in good quality on my crt. Kodi also has some nice addons that let me watch youtube, twitch, and some other stuff i probably wont use.


Moonlight just lets me stream my PC to the pi which will be neat for playing some newer stuff that looks nice on a CRT. i plan on playing bomb rush cyberfunk this way. although im still trying to get it to fit properly on my screen.
