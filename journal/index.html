QUITTING MY JOB // 9.2.25

Yeahhhh ive only worked 4 days, and I'm already quitting. not a good look especially since its my first job but I just should not have gotten a job with the other stuff going on, and maybe should have looked for a less demanding place/position. I thought having a job would help me feel less hollow about the girl stuff, but it just made it worse, and that situation on its own got worse too. I found out she also hid me from seeing her insta story, which I dont care about insta but she made the effort to do that so idk maybe im cooked. she has still yet to reply, so still no clue whats going on.

Aside from that though, the job is just way more work than its worth. my friends do less and get paid more, and shouldn't be any harder to get a job like theirs. There were a lot of issues with this job that just made me decide it's not the time for me. It was my first job and they had me working 5 days in a row, despite being part time so they just took advantage of the week change to make me work 5 consecutive days and I just was not ready for that. That's the smallest issue but it was demoralizing from the start. Really the big problem was they had me opening by myself on my 2nd, 3rd, and 4th day. Since I was the guy that takes in donations at goodwill, I'm supposed to have 1 or 2 other people helping me sort through stuff cause the bins will fill up really fast. But they just had me doing it alone for a couple hours at the busiest time of day, so I was constantly taking donations in and never having time to sort them. It's very overwhelming and it's just impossible to empty the bins before more people come in. And a lot of people just donate stuff they arent allowed to donate when I tell them they can't, then my coworker comes in and says we cant take that stuff like yeah I know man. If it payed better I would be able to endure this but its minimum wage and I have to deal with old white people and sorting through garbage bags of people donations.

I still come out of this in a better spot though I think. I'm gonna take some time to deal with the girl stuff, and then try to get a car so I don't need to take the bus, then spend more time looking for a job that suits me better. I just applied here since it was where my brother worked, but I should have taken more time and made a more informed decision.

ZEN AND MONKLIKE // 8.27.25

Hello again buddies, new layout still isn't done, but I wanna yap for a bit since a lot has been going on. I've been dealing with a great mental turmoil over the past month, and usually when stuff like this happens I go zen and monklike mode. Meaning I pick out all the things I do that I shouldn't and try to fix them. Originally, I first went zen and monklike mode 2 years ago, just after I had been dealing with a lot of stuff over that year and never really handled any of it, so it just crumbled and I stopped talking to everyone and started paying more attention to my physical health. The not talking to anyone thing was definitely unhealthy, and I'm not doing that now, but it was how I dealt with that at the time.

Fast forward to this month, This girl I've been half with for almost a year brought up being unsure about being together, and then she got sick immediately after and can't respond. So I've been in this limbo state where I don't know what's gonna happen, since we haven't gotten to talk about it for about a month. This obviously took a pretty big toll on me in the first place, but now letting that sadness boil for a month its just gotten so much worse. I don't blame her obviously, she didn't choose to get sick, it just really hurts to get such drastic news and then no elaboration for a month, and who knows how much longer it will be. We did talk for a few minutes a couple weeks ago, but it didn't give much solace.

Anyways, my response to this has been overall pretty beneficiary to me, aside from when I'm rotting. I've gone back to full zen and monklike mode, I'm not drinking anything but water, I've been working out again, and eating healthier. But most importantly, I finally got my first job. Immediately after I got that first message, I kinda panicked and decided I needed to get my life together somewhat, cause if she leaves I really have nothing going for me. I don't put relationships on this big pedestal, I actually hate people that do that, but this was the first time it felt different. I would be jobless, carless, and single while living in my parents house. I did not want that so I applied to be a goodwill donation attendant and got the job hooray!!! Obviously, it's not gonna get me enough money to move out or anything but at least its something, and I'll be able to put it on my resume for my next job. My first day is tomorrow, I'm a little nervous, I am not good in social situations, but the only way to fix that is just throw myself in one so we'll see.

I'm still holding on to hope that me and her aren't over, she said some things that make it seem like it might not be, so I'm not gonna completely give up until she's better and responds. Will give updates thank you for reading my #VentPost
