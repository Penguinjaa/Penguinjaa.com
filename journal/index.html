v5.55 PROGRESS REPORT // 11.24.24

the journal is back!!!! a couple months ago, i got pretty fed up with my current website, so i wanted more blog posts or just personal writing in general on here. but i dont like the idea of muddying up my blog with just life updates, since i wanna use the blog for more project stuff like my crt media center raspberry pi. so i decided to add a journal again which i had on my very ugly v1.11 of this website. hopefully now less ugly and more cool.

anyway, i just intend to ramble about life on here, and share amazing vibes!!! right now im listening to that new kendrick album and taking a brief break from javascript. as of right now, the home page is (mostly) done, and the journal and media log are completely done. im pretty happy with how the media log turned out especially, i now have a section to write about the movie or whatever so i can give my thoughts. i did have this before but i wasnt able to find a way to fit reviews and make it look good/be functional. now that im trying to make most pages unique instead of just piecing them together like legos, i was able to make it work.

also, i moved the theme switcher into button that sit on the bottom right of the header. OH and i havent mentioned the marquee!!! i mentioned earlier that i got fed up a couple months ago with my layout, but i was struggling to make a new one i liked. then i had an idea to make a red lcd marquee like the one from the akira screenshot i use as a banner for the neo-tokyo theme. im really happy with how it turned out, i think its a really cool way to display some information, and its fun to include a randomized splash text.

thats basically the extent of what ive done so far, this entry is longer than they will usually be just wanted to get down a first post and write out what ive done. thanks for reading buddy!!!
