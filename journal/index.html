V5.55 PROGRESS REPORT 2 // 7.6.25

wow i cannot believe that last entry is from 7 months ago!!!! i still remember writing it like it was a month ago but i suppose ive been a bit of a busy bee. i was slacking on website updates for a bit i had a big motivation burst at the beginning of the year and then stopped mostly until a month ago and ive been slowly sprinkling updates.

i am currently listening to my website playlist, mostly daft punk and the synthy kanye songs, as well as lupe fiasco's food & liqour, ive been obsessed with that album lately. i mentioned in the last entry that i was really happy with the media log, but i suppose that was a lie cause i completely re-did it again and now i think i am actually happy with it. i still need to figure out where to put some buttons and stuff but its turning out really nice so far. especially a fan of the grid/list view option, i think ive found a good middle ground of being able to write long reviews for stuff but not feeling forced to in order to not make the page look empty.

i also rewrote the vinyl page, i never put it out the way it was but i wrote it in like a fugue state a year ago and it was really poorly made. it looked really good though so i finally came back and rewrote it and now it works on all displays (i think) and looks super cool. its themed after this game called Bomb Rush Cyberfunk, i highly recommend you play it if you havent i promise you will love it.

ive been really really trying to figure out how i want the home page to look, and i think im finally starting to get somewhere, but i change my mind so often we will see. maybe ill be writing here in a year still not decided who knows. but even if i redesign it again, ive implemented some stuff im really happy with. im using lanyard to pull information from my discord account to display on my profile section. which means my little profile picture is pulled from my discord, it shows a little dot with my discord online status, and it even will display the game im playing if i have one open. its actually really cool and ive had my eye on it for a bit but never looked into putting it on my site until now. i dont think ive seen someone do this before at least on nekoweb or neocities, but i would highly recommend you try it!!!

as far as my personal life goes im pretty geeked!!! ive been talking to my buddies a lot and i feel like ive cultivated a more positive social circle for myself, a lot less evil sinister spirits coming to stab and kill me. the nintendo switch 2 came out since last writing as well, i know there was a lot of controversy but i didnt care at all i bought it day 1 of course and im really really liking it. mario kart world is super fun the free roam surprised me with how much time im spending in it. i think im at 50 hours or something? 100%ing it is gonna be a challenge but im most of the way there. very VERY excited for donkey kong bananza, that game looks incredible. i also helped my buddy make a portfolio website for her college class, heres the link if you wanna check it out, i think its epic and she cooked.

v5.55 PROGRESS REPORT // 11.24.24

the journal is back!!!! a couple months ago, i got pretty fed up with my current website, so i wanted more blog posts or just personal writing in general on here. but i dont like the idea of muddying up my blog with just life updates, since i wanna use the blog for more project stuff like my crt media center raspberry pi. so i decided to add a journal again which i had on my very ugly v1.11 of this website. hopefully now less ugly and more cool.

anyway, i just intend to ramble about life on here, and share amazing vibes!!! right now im listening to that new kendrick album and taking a brief break from javascript. as of right now, the home page is (mostly) done, and the journal and media log are completely done. im pretty happy with how the media log turned out especially, i now have a section to write about the movie or whatever so i can give my thoughts. i did have this before but i wasnt able to find a way to fit reviews and make it look good/be functional. now that im trying to make most pages unique instead of just piecing them together like legos, i was able to make it work.

also, i moved the theme switcher into button that sit on the bottom right of the header. OH and i havent mentioned the marquee!!! i mentioned earlier that i got fed up a couple months ago with my layout, but i was struggling to make a new one i liked. then i had an idea to make a red lcd marquee like the one from the akira screenshot i use as a banner for the neo-tokyo theme. im really happy with how it turned out, i think its a really cool way to display some information, and its fun to include a randomized splash text.
