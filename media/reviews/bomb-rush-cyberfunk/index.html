BOMB RUSH CYBERFUNK REVIEW

BOMB RUSH CYBERFUNK

96/100

[BUY ON STEAM]

Bomb Rush Cyberfunk is a spiritual successor of sorts to Jet Set Radio, although i am a poser so i havent played JSR so i cant tell you if they did a good job at spiritually successing it. I can say though that this is one of my favorite games ive played in a long time.


it does a really good job at capturing that early 2000s/gamecube era vibe. im kinda a geek for that stuff so it takes a lot for me to say something like that . if you put it in 4:3 and on a crt it would not be out of place with other games of that era.
